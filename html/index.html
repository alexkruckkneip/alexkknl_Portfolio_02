Hi,

 my name is Alexander and I have been a digital designer with heart and soul for more than two decades. For 6 of those years I was able to hire and lead four Product/­UR/­UX/­UI Design Teams, national and international, in three different countries and two languages.

In each leadership role I had the opportunity to develop all design processes from scratch with key stakeholders from different departments.

I founded my first product design team for Europe's most successful freight exchange platform TimoCom in Ger­many. The key to the rapid success of applying the brand design to two existing products and launching two completely new products within four years was the successful introduction of the designers' new skill to work in code with the development of the design system.

We were finally getting regular qualitative feedback from B2B clients with my second design team, because I was able to persuade teammates in the sales department to become user researchers. A new milestone for the third design team was empowering my colleagues to truly bring users from every continent in front of the camera for our first global user research. Helping a UI designer in my fourth design team to grow in UX Design and run regular unmoderated testing in Maze was a key success factor in massively increasing conversion.

All challenges needed their individual UX UI strategy to be successful. I've compiled some hands on UX and UI design strategy stories on this self-crafted website that might inspire you for your next big move.

Always happy to help ;-) Alexander KK
