Hi,

 my name is Alexander, I am originally from Germany and have been a digital designer with heart and soul for more than two decades. I was lucky enough to be able to live and work in four European countries with the support of my family. This gave me the opportunity to develop the best design solutions for a wide range of companies and customers around the world.

I've compiled some hands on UX and UI design strategy case studies on this self-crafted website. The focus is on how I have successfully achieved key business goals for very diverse companies and users.

In all cases I had to do something beyond what was expected. I have summarized what that was under the heading 'Inspire for more'.

Always happy to help ;-) Alexander KK

Top case studies,

UX UI Strategy
Save the bot

As a solo Senior UX Designer on the cross-functional AI team, I took charge of the design process at a critical stage. This is how I helped the product team and management agree on a final version of their brand new bot authoring software. This was the key success driver to enable Genesys to sell a first version for a two digit million dollar price only 9 months after I started.

UX UI Strategy
Uncover growth markets

By introducing new UX design tools and processes, I helped TimoCom, Europe's largest online freight exchange platform, enter new growth markets.

UX UI & AB Tests
AB tests at Rentalcars

I established new UX methodologies that helped the cross-functional team increase the rate of successful A/B experiments. As such, I have been primarily responsible for increasing many of the conversion rates resulting in tens of millions of Pounds in annual revenue growth for Rentalcars.com.

Work Experience

6 years People Management

I have 6 years experience as people manager with a maximum of 6 reports at the same time. In this role I coached junior and senior designers, briefed freelancers and hired designers from agencies. As a hiring manager, I have found and hired talent nationally and globally in four countries and two languages.

11 years UX Design

In 2012 I passed the exam to become a certified usability engineer FIT in Bonn. At that time, this further training was for professionals who had demonstrably worked in product development for at least two years. My Credential ID is UE-1111--04. Since then, I have regularly introduced and used the methods of UX design in the form of user research, design thinking, lean design and design sprints.

13 years Product Design

In 2010 I started as the first Product Designer at Europe's largest freight exchange TimoCom. Since then, I have always been responsible for the entire production process related to design. I repeatedly introduced shortcuts that increased the quality and speed of the design process. Such improvements included, for example, the introduction and development of multiple design systems as well as the direct creation of prototypes in code using HTML, CSS, Node, SASS, JS, Angular or PHP.

20 years Digital Designer

After graduating as a media designer for digital and print in 2003, I worked for many national and international companies in Germany. Even during my time as a UX and product designer from 2010 onwards, the skills I learned always helped me to achieve more. As a digital designer, I have used experience and skills in the areas of 3D design, graphic design, print and web design.
