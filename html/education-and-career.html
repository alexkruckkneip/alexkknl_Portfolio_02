Education

1997
Abitur at the Max Planck Gymnasium in Gelsenkirchen Buer

In 1997 I graduated from the Max Planck Gymnasium in Gelsenkirchen-Buer. My advanced courses were physics and mathematics.

2003
Media designer for digital and print

Completion of the recognized training as a media designer for digital and print media at the Kartäserwall vocational college in Cologne in 2003.

The training lasted 2 1/2 years and was a combination of practical work in a company and learning at the vocational school.

2012
Certified Usability Engineer
Fraunhofer FIT

Issued Feb 2012
Credential ID UE-1111--04

“In our vocational training, we provide you with the essential tools for a profound consulting activity in usability engineering. You will learn the concepts, methods, and procedures of usability engineering in a practice-oriented manner.” [usability-ux.fit.fraunhofer.de in 2023]

After almost 2 years as the first product designer at Europe's largest freight exchange TimoCom, I wanted to bring our customers more into the center of my work.

At that time, the Fraunhofer Institute in Bonn offered further training to become a usability engineer specifically for professionals with experience in product development.

I was very fortunate that the TimoCom management supported me financially in this further development.
