Jaja Finance

Head of UX UI
Remote, Mar 2022 - Jan 2023

Company Profile

UK, London
Fintech, Scale-up, B2C

About

“Recharge your online credit safely & securely at recharge.com. Get a digital prepaid code for your favourite entertainment service, game or online shop, or top up your mobile phone. It's fast, safe & simple.”
[recharge.com/en-gb in 2021]

Achievements

Within 6 months I was asked to build a complete UX/UI Design infrastructure ready to onboard our new partners Asda and IHG, the International Hotel Group, from scratch. The last Head of UX UI had left Jaja 6 months earlier without any UX documentation.

In these first six months one UI designer and I had to deliver for three development teams on up to five different products, plus the commercial and marketing departments.

By helping her grow as a designer and making basic iterative improvements in Figma, we were able to make regular, repetitive design changes in minutes instead of hours. In addition, I built a simple framework that also enabled her to generate qualitative data with unmoderated user tests in Maze.

The impressed IT platform dev leads asked me to lead the devel-opment of the desperately needed design system and so I organised regular workshops. At the same time, I hired a new UX designer internationally and introduced a lean efficient competitor analysis system.

Achievements

Recharge is a company in a transitioning from a start up to a scale up. I helped to develop the small European portfolio of brands to a global market leader.

In my role as Senior UX Design Lead I created and executed the UX strategy for Recharge.com with the leadership team and key stakeholders.

This includes international hiring, team building and coaching together with design system implementation, design thinking workshops and global moderated user testing.

Genesys

Senior UX Designer AI
Onsite, Mar 2019 - Aug 2020

Company Profile

IR, Galway
Saas, Enterprise, B2B

About

“Every year, Genesys delivers more than 70 billion remarkable customer experiences for organizations in over 100 countries. Through the power of the cloud and AI, our technology connects every customer moment across marketing, sales & service on any channel, while also improving employee experiences.”
[genesys.com in 2021]

Achievements

These days bots are the biggest innovation to decrease the costs for Genesys customers in the service industry. The problem was that in 2019 bot authoring software was only operable by high skilled people. Within 9 months, Genesys wanted to sell a full working bot authoring software that was easy to use for nearly everyone.

I successfully established international UX partnerships and lined up regular user testing sessions to reach and keep our promise "…and all easy to use". With my innovative way of working, together with the teams and product management, my design driven approach was a key success factor to get a first multi million dollar contract for our bot authoring software in January 2020.

Starting at Maginus was like jumping into cold water. Before it was possible to launch a really ambitious UX program, I first had to help in an emergency situation to save the deal with a very important customer.

Now, it was time to move on and establish UX Design at Maginus. Creating a new design framework, with the support of a well known agency from Manchester, was an awesome experience.

To establish new ideation processes, I organised new design thinking sessions about topics like information architecture and A/B testing for ecommerce platforms.

Rentalcars

UX UI Designer
Onsite, Nov 2015 - Mar 2018

UK, Manchester

About

“At Rentalcars.com everything we do is about giving you the freedom to discover more. We’ll move mountains to find you the right rental car, and bring you a smooth, hassle-free experience from start to finish.”
[rentalcars.com/us/about/ in 2021]

Achievements

When I joined Rentalcars, a highly efficient A/B experiment platform was the key driver for success.

My task was to establish new UX processes that helped the development team to increase the ratio of successful experiments. By improving the existing idea process by supporting Devs and PO with new UX methods, I guided the team to multiple successful experiments. Cross collaboration with a top user research team helped to find the right topics.

Also learning from other successful platforms introducing com-petitor analysis led to a higher positive experiment ratio. Mockup sessions with cross functional teams found innovative new ideas to get even more bpd and multi million more Pound Sterling revenue for Rentalcars.com.
